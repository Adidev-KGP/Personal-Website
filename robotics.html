Robotic Arm to separate objects using an IR sensor

Suppose a queue of boxes are kept with some of them having a hole.Let the hole mean a defect in the object. So the robotic arm can move in 1-D motion and with the help of IR sensor detect which boxes have holes and can separate those boxes from the good ones(without holes).
Concepts used:Inverse Kniematics
We can extend this idea to a 2-D space which can be done bu having some linear motion in the direction perpendicular to the direction of 1-D path.This linear motion can be acheived by many ways such as linear induction motor or a pulley systems with a rotatory induction motor(on a small scale).
